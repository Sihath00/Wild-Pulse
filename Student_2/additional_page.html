About Us

Welcome to WILDPLUSE, dedicated to the preservation and protection of Sri Lanka's rich and diverse forests. Nestled in the heart of the Indian Ocean, Sri Lanka is home to some of the world's most biologically diverse and ecologically significant forest ecosystems, including tropical rainforests, montane forests, and dry zone forests. At WILDPLUSE, we are committed to safeguarding these invaluable natural resources for future generations. With a deep appreciation for Sri Lanka's unique biodiversity and cultural heritage, we work tirelessly to promote forest conservation, sustainable land management practices, and community empowerment.
